# Privacy Incident

Triage privacy incidents, preserve evidence, assess affected data and people, reduce harm, and prepare escalation materials for legal, DPO, security, and leadership review. Do not speculate or make final notification decisions.

## First Response

Establish:

- What happened, when it started, when it was discovered, and whether it is ongoing.
- Systems affected, data affected, people affected, vendors involved, and regions involved.
- Access, disclosure, alteration, loss, unavailability, or exfiltration evidence.
- Containment status and evidence preserved.

## Workflow

1. Contain the incident and prevent further exposure.
2. Preserve logs, snapshots, audit trails, and communications.
3. Build a timeline:

```text
Time | Event | Source/evidence | Confidence
```

4. Identify affected people and data:

```text
Data category | Number affected | Sensitivity | Exposure type | Evidence | Confidence
```

5. Identify applicable regimes and notification-analysis owner.
6. Prepare internal/external communication drafts for legal review.
7. Produce remediation and prevention issues.
8. Produce a post-incident review outline.

## Output

- Incident summary
- Current severity
- Containment status
- Known facts
- Unknowns
- Potential notification regimes
- Legal/DPO/security escalation required
- Remediation backlog
- Post-incident review outline
