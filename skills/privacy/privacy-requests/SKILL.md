# Privacy Requests

Design rights workflows that are operationally and technically feasible across primary systems, vendors, logs, backups, exports, and support processes. This is not legal advice; verify current regime-specific deadlines and exceptions from official sources or user-provided legal materials.

## Workflow

1. Identify applicable regimes and rights.
2. Map identity verification and authorised-agent requirements.
3. Map lookup sources across databases, logs, files, vendors, warehouses, support tools, and backups.
4. Define intake, fulfilment, exception, appeal/escalation, and audit workflows.
5. Define vendor actions and evidence retention.
6. Create implementation issues.

## Rights To Consider

- Access / know
- Deletion / erasure
- Correction / rectification
- Portability
- Restriction
- Objection
- Consent withdrawal
- Opt out of sale/sharing
- Limit use/disclosure of sensitive personal information
- Automated decision-making explanation, access, opt-out, or review where applicable

## Output

```text
Right | Applies? | Intake path | Verification | Systems touched | Vendor actions | Deadline source | Exceptions | Audit evidence | Gaps
```

For implementation issues include:

- User story
- Data systems involved
- Acceptance criteria
- Test cases
- Audit log requirement
- Manual fallback
- Legal/privacy review needed
